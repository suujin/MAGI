#SUMMARY
##TITLE 
AKT1 Mutations in Non-Small Cell Lung Cancer (NSCLC)
##TEXT
Somatic mutations in AKT1 have been found in ~ 1% of all NSCLC ([http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/18504432 Bleeker, 2008]; [http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/18611285 Do, 2008]; [http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/18256540 Malanga, 2008]), in both adenocarcinoma and squamous cell carcinoma histology. Specific clinical characteristics of lung cancer patients harboring AKT1 mutations have yet to be described.
